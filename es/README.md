# Entrepreneurship & Startup (ES)

> **Subject Code:** DI04000081  
> **Credits:** 4 | **Hours:** 45 (L:3, T:0, PR:0)  
> **Marks:** Theory 70 + PA 30 + Internal 20 + Viva 30 = 150

---

## Course Outcomes

| #   | Outcome                                                       | Level            |
| --- | ------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| CO1 | Understand and apply key entrepreneurial traits and behaviors | Understand/Apply |
| CO2 | Apply market research and business modeling techniques        | Apply            |
| CO3 | Analyze sources of funding and government support schemes     | Analyze          |
| CO4 | Apply marketing strategies for a new venture                  | Apply            |
| CO5 | Create a project proposal and understand exit strategies      | Create           |
